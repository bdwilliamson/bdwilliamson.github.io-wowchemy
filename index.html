Brian David Williamson

 

I'm currently a fourth-year PhD student in the Department of Biostatistics at the University of Washington.

My dissertation work is with Marco Carone and Noah Simon. We develop nonparametric methods for possibly high-dimensional data, with applications in HIV/AIDS.

My RA work is with Jim Hughes, Deborah Donnell, and Brett Hanscom. We work on methods for rigorously adapting non-inferiority margins under observable non-constancy, particularly in HIV prevention clinical trials.
